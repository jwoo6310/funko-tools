Where to pull comps

eBay sold listings (filter → Sold + the exact Pop #) is the truth source. With an eBay Store, Terapeak in Seller Hub gives you median/avg sold for free — that's your “median” field. PriceCharting is a fast loose-value sanity check. Median > average here, since one crazy sale skews the mean.
